# The Origami Bird

**Difficulty:** Medium
**Category:** Forensics / Stego

## Mission Briefing
The Professor always plans ten steps ahead. During the chaos at the Royal Mint of Spain, Inspector Murillo's forensics team found this simple paper origami bird left deliberately on a desk. 

We managed to get a high-resolution scan of it before it was booked into evidence. It looks like an ordinary piece of folded paper, but the Professor never leaves anything behind by accident. 

Can you unfold the secrets hidden within?
